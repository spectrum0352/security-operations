
**Signs of DDoS attack**
DDoS attacks target the availability of a system rather than compromising it directly. Here are some signs to watch out for:
* Unusual Network Traffic: A sudden and significant surge in traffic overwhelming your network bandwidth.
* Slow Website/Service: Users experiencing slow loading times or complete inaccessibility of your website or online services.
* Service Disruptions: Unexplained errors, timeouts, and outages across various network services.
* Increased CPU/Memory Usage: Spikes in server resource consumption due to handling excessive traffic.

For a more comprehensive detection approach, consider these additional factors:
* Traffic Source Analysis: Look for suspicious patterns like traffic originating from a single IP address or location.
* Attack Type Identification: Different DDoS attacks have distinct characteristics. Analyze traffic patterns to identify the specific attack method.
* Behavioural Analysis: Monitor user behaviour for anomalies. A DDoS attack might involve automated bots instead of genuine user traffic.
