Incident Detection Steps

Monitoring and Logging: Continuously monitor systems for suspicious activities using security tools and protocols.
Alerting: Generate alerts whenever anomalies or events exceeding pre-defined thresholds occur.
Analysis: Evaluate alerts to assess their legitimacy and potential severity.
Triaging: Prioritize alerts based on potential impact and urgency.
Containment: Take initial actions to limit potential damage, such as blocking access or isolating affected systems.
